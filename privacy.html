Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ADONE (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit adone.agency or submit a launch review request.

1. Who we are

ADONE is an independent performance marketing agency providing Google Ads and Meta (Facebook / Instagram) launch and scaling services. We are not affiliated with Google or Meta.

2. Data we collect

When you contact us, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Advertising channel (Google Ads, Meta Ads, or both)
  • Business vertical and estimated monthly ad spend
  • Telegram or WhatsApp username / phone number you provide
  • Preferred messenger
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device, pages visited, and cookies (see Cookie Policy)

3. How we use your data

  • To respond to your inquiry (typically within 24 hours)
  • To review your vertical, geo, and eligibility for our services
  • To prepare proposals and onboarding
  • To process top-ups, refunds, and billing where applicable
  • To improve our website and comply with legal obligations

4. Legal basis (EEA/UK)

We process data based on your consent (form submission), steps before a contract, performance of a contract, and legitimate interest in operating our agency.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with hosting providers, payment processors, and messengers you choose (e.g. Telegram or WhatsApp) only as needed to deliver our services.

6. International transfers

If data is processed outside your country, we use appropriate safeguards where required by law.

7. Retention

Inquiry and client data is kept as long as needed for business, tax, and legal purposes, then deleted or anonymized.

8. Your rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of processing, and object to certain processing where applicable. Contact us using the details on the main site.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Children

Our services are for businesses. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

11. Contact

For privacy requests, contact ADONE via the form on our homepage, Telegram, or WhatsApp.
